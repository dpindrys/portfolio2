Matching Mental Models
When redesigning the visual collaboration piece of the software, I looked to how it all started: as posters on a physical wall.

I made a tappable prototype of the in-forum experience in Keynote that we tested on an iPad with Conteneo users. They were super excited to see the new design, but less excited about a few of the design choices I made to move toward a minimal UI.

Some things noted from testing:

  • chat was super important - don't hide. ever.
  • while being able to make regions with a pen tool was embraced, it was slow
  • loved being able to see who was in the forum
  • really liked being able to use entire screen
Luke goes on and on about the power of the platform. Weave makes that so much more transparent...
